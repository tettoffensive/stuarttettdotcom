Doppler's Dogpatch is a new premium ice cream brand in North Carolina.

They wanted their brand to be centered around their dog, Doppler. They wanted a minimal and modern logo to be used on their pints and marketing materials.

After some loose sketching, I decided to create a logo with a profile view. I created the dog head (and eventually the hat and ice cream scoop) entirely from circles.

I played around with different ways of displaying the dog's profile.

I decided to try adding a scoop of ice cream on the snout to better represent what the product is in a cute way.

After iterating with the client a couple of times we ended up with a green and brown-gray color scheme. With a little splash of pink for the ice cream.

BuildUp.tv is an online platform centered around the Maker/D.I.Y. community. I sketched several concepts, but this logo with the wood-cut ends stood out with it's simplicity and representation of the brand.

SousChef is a mobile recipe app that I cofounded. I was inspired by the artwork from Lucky Peach magazine (R.I.P.), which is a little more rough and grungy than the current trends in mobile products. I wanted a bold color which stood out amongst apps on the iPhone's home screen and also didn't make the splash look like blood.
