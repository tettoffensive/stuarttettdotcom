About

Stuart has a unique background with a double major in Art & Design and Computer Science, which give way to a rare skill-set of strong visual and analytical problem-solving skills. He spent 6 years with DreamWorks Animation as an Effects Animator prior to joining Grid (YC S12), where he excelled quickly and transitioned into a CTO role within his first 6 months. He continued developing mobile apps at Swipe Labs for 2 years. But he missed the creative freedom of doing visual work and is now concentrating on design.

He is now available for hire for freelance for designing logos, branding, UI/UX for mobile and web.
