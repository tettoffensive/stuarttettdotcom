Hello! My name is Stuart. I have a mixed background in design and development.

From an early age, I was always creating art. I began coding in middle school back in the 90s. I got into making websites and even into 3d animation.

I ended up doing a double major in Art & Design and Computer Science.

Then I got my Master's in a combo of those two disciplines called Visualization Science.

I've gone from creating visual effects for film to developing digital products on the mobile and web.

I loved using my visual creativity to craft fire on films like How To Train Your Dragon, but I was drawn back to the product world when the iPhone came out.

Since then, I've been working at early-stage startups working on mobile and web products.

I've had the opportunity to wear many hats on these small teams. I've found that my strengths are around:

Building a great experience for people using digital interfaces.

Matching the user's needs with business needs to create lasting growth.

Crafting interfaces that are functional and aesthetically pleasing.
